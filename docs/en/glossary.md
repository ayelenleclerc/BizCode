# Glossary

## Domain Terms

### A

**ARCA** (Argentina)
: *Agencia de Recaudación y Control Aduanero* — Argentina’s federal tax authority (successor to the former AFIP). Issues CUIT numbers and regulates electronic invoicing.

**Artículo / Product**
: An item sold by the business. Has a code, description, price lists, VAT condition, and stock level.

### C

**CF — Consumidor Final / End Consumer**
: A customer who is not registered for VAT. Receives Factura B; does not pay VAT separately (it is included in the price).

**Condición IVA / VAT Condition (Customer)**
: The customer's tax registration status: RI, Monotributo, CF, or Exento. Determines how VAT is calculated on invoices.

**Condición IVA (Artículo) / VAT Rate (Product)**
: The applicable VAT rate for a product: 21%, 10.5%, or Exempt.

**CUIT / CUIL**
: Clave Única de Identificación Tributaria / Laboral. Argentine 11-digit tax ID. Format: `XX-XXXXXXXX-X`. Validated with a modulo-11 check digit algorithm.

### E

**Exento / Exempt**
: A customer or product category not subject to VAT.

### F

**Factura A / Invoice A**
: Invoice issued to registered VAT taxpayers (RI). Shows VAT amount separately. Allows the customer to claim VAT credit.

**Factura B / Invoice B**
: Invoice issued to end consumers (CF) and Monotributo taxpayers. VAT is included in the total price; not shown separately.

### M

**Monotributo / Simplified Tax Regime**
: Argentine simplified tax regime for small taxpayers. They pay a flat monthly fee instead of VAT and income tax. When buying, they receive Factura B but the seller charges VAT as if selling to CF.

### N

**Neto / Net Amount**
: The taxable base before VAT. `Neto 21%` is the subtotal of items with 21% VAT; `Neto 10.5%` for 10.5% items; `Neto Exento` for exempt items.

### R

**RI — Responsable Inscripto / Registered VAT Taxpayer**
: A business registered for VAT. Receives Factura A. Can claim VAT credit on purchases.

**Rubro / Category**
: A product category used to classify articles (e.g., "Herramientas", "Electrónica").

### U

**U. Medida / Unit of Measure**
: The unit in which a product is sold (e.g., `U` = unit, `kg` = kilogram, `l` = litre).
